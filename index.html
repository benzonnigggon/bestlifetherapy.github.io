Fees & Session Info

Counselling sessions take place in my private practice via telehealth such as Skype, Zoom and face to face. Generally sessions go for 50 minutes, sessions that go over without prior booking will incur additional fees as they take extra time away from subsequent clients.

  • Individual Counselling (50 minutes) $110
  • Sliding scale fees are available for eligible clients.
  • Pro bono/minimum fee sessions are available for those on low income and special considerations for asylum seekers, Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander folks, people of colour, students and person with special needs.
  • All fees are payable in CASH (with receipt), via bank transfer or by invoice.
  • Cancellation Policy: To cancel or reschedule contact me at least 24 hours prior to appointment start time.
